Some personal experiences

Nepal earthquake, 2015

  1. Map villages and roads using satellite imagery
  2. Map destroyed buildings using aerial imagery
  3. Map firecamps and places to land helicopters
  4. Reach the villages and provide help

PoliMappers, student association

Malaria eradication

200+ children (10~12 y.o.) involved in mapping!

  1. Map villages and roads using satellite imagery
  2. Run spatial models to compute risk
  3. Take action on high risk areas

Protect girls from Female Genital Mutilation

  1. Map villages and roads using satellite imagery
  2. Visit the villages and educate people

Protect girls from Female Genital Mutilation

2017 Results (Serengeti, Tanzania):

  • 1300 online volunteers, 200 local mappers
  • 1.1 million building and 64.000 km of roads added
  • Prevented 2257 girls from being cut
  • 4 girls died (previous year: 12)
